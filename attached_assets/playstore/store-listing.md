### Long Description (max 4000 chars)
```
MtG Master is your German-first companion app for Magic: The Gathering – fast, focused, and free of ad clutter.

★ CARD SEARCH
Search any Magic card in German or English with live suggestions. View high-res images, mana costs, types, abilities, format legality (Standard, Pioneer, Modern, Legacy, Commander) and similar cards. One tap takes you to Scryfall for prices, printings and rulings, or to Cardmarket to buy.

★ KEYWORDS & RULES
Over 130 Magic keywords explained in full German – plus a Rules FAQ with 40+ ready-made Q&A cards in 6 categories, searchable, with a clear verdict on every ruling.

★ DECK BUILDER
Build decks right inside the app. Mana-curve and speed analysis show you at a glance where your deck shines and where to tune. Categorised synergy groups with card images and detail modals make fine-tuning easy.

★ DECK IDEAS
40+ ready-made archetypes for Modern, Standard, Pioneer, Commander and Pauper – each with a complete sample decklist. Plus 20 complete Commander decks (~100 cards each) to draw inspiration from or copy outright.

★ COMBO DATABASE
Direct access to Commander Spellbook: tap a card and instantly see every combo it enables.

★ MATCHUP MODE
Compare your deck to a friend's deck. The matchup tool runs heuristic and Monte-Carlo simulations and shows you the win rate plus concrete tips to improve.

★ FRIENDS
Connect with other players and selectively share individual decks for the matchup mode.

★ FAVORITES & HISTORY
Save your favourite cards and revisit recent searches anytime – synced across devices (optional).

★ CLOUD SYNC
With a free account (email login, no password needed) you sync decks, favourites and history between phone, tablet and web browser.

★ GERMAN FIRST
All card text and keywords are primarily in German. Switch to English with a single tap.

★ NO TRACKING CIRCUS
No hidden trackers, no forced sign-up, no ad bombardment. Open for everyone, lightweight, fast.

Magic: The Gathering and all card names are trademarks of Wizards of the Coast LLC. MtG Master is an independent fan project and not officially affiliated with Wizards of the Coast or Hasbro. Card data and images come from the Scryfall API.
```
